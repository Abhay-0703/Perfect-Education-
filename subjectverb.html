Subject-Verb Agreement

Rule 1:

सभी subjects के नाम singular होते हैं। इनके साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जायेगी।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
Politics is an interesting subject. ✔ Politics are an interesting subject. ✘
Mathematics is a difficult subject. ✔ Mathematics are a difficult subject. ✘
The news is on at 9 PM. ✔ The news are on at 9 PM. ✘
The information is useful. ✔ The information are useful. ✘
Gymnastics is a popular sport. ✔ Gymnastics are a popular sport. ✘

Rule 2:

सभी देशों के नाम singular होते हैं तथा इनके साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जायेगी।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
India is my country. ✔ India are my country. ✘
Sri Lanka is our neighbour country. ✔ Sri Lanka are our neighbour country. ✘
Japan is known for its technology. ✔ Japan are known for its technology. ✘
Canada is famous for its natural beauty. ✔ Canada are famous for its natural beauty. ✘
Russia is the largest country in the world. ✔ Russia are the largest country in the world. ✘

Note: लेकिन किसी भी जगह का नाम उसके किसी भी समूह के लिये use किया जाय तो उसे Plural मानेंगे।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
India are to win the match. ✔ India is to win the match. ✘
England are leading the tournament. ✔ England is leading the tournament. ✘
Italy are hosting the event. ✔ Italy is hosting the event. ✘

Rule 3

सभी पुस्तकों के नाम singular होते हैं तथा इनके साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जाती है।

The Mid Summer's Dream are my favourite book. ✘
The Mid Summer's Dream is my favourite book. ✔

Rule 4

सभी बीमारियों के नाम singular होते हैं तथा इनके साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जाती है।

Cancer is a dangerous disease. ✔
Cancer are a dangerous disease. ✘

Rule 5

सभी खेलों के नाम singular होते हैं तथा इनके साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जायेगी।

Darts is my favourite game. ✔
Cricket is played in all over the world. ✔

Rule 6

Material nouns तथा Abstract Nouns हमेशा singular होते हैं या इनके साथ use होने वाली verb singular होती है।

Water is life. ✔
Honesty is the best policy. ✔
Gold is a precious metal. ✔
Gold and Silver are precious metals. ✔
Childhood and boyhood are golden times of life. ✔

Rule 7

कुछ Nouns ऐसे होते हैं, जो हमेशा Pair में use किये जाते हैं उनके साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जाती है।

Slow and steady always win. ✘
Slow and steady always wins. ✔
Your bag and baggage are ready so you may go now. ✘
Your bag and baggage is ready so you may go now. ✔

Rule 8

यदि किसी sentence में each/every + singular countable noun + and + each/every singular countable noun या each / every + singular countable noun + and + singular countable noun आ रहा हो, तो इनके साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जायेगी।

Each man and each woman are ready to do everything for the nation. ✘
Each man and each woman is ready to do everything for the nation. ✔
Each student and teacher is to attend the programme. ✔
Each student and teacher are to attend the programme. ✘

Rule 9

सभी Indefinite pronoun (Everyone, everybody, everything, no one, nobody, nothing, anyone, anybody, anything, someone, somebody, something, one) के साथ हमेशा singular verb use की जाती है।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
Nothing is impossible. ✔ Nothing are impossible. ✘
Everyone wants to help me in my bad days. ✔ Everyone want to help me in my bad days. ✘
Everything is in its place. ✔ Everything are in its place. ✘
Something is wrong here. ✔ Something are wrong here. ✘
Nothing has changed since last year. ✔ Nothing have changed since last year. ✘
Anybody is welcome to join us. ✔ Anybody are welcome to join us. ✘
Somebody has left their bag here. ✔ Somebody have left their bag here. ✘
Everyone is attending the meeting. ✔ Everyone are attending the meeting. ✘
Nothing is more important than health. ✔ Nothing are more important than health. ✘
Anyone is allowed to enter the room. ✔ Anyone are allowed to enter the room. ✘

Rule 10

यदि either या neither को किसी sentence के subject के रूप में use किये जाय तो हमेशा singular verb use की जायेगी।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
There are two books here but either is useful to me. ✔ There are two books here but either are useful to me. ✘
Both my friends are rich but neither is intelligent. ✔ Both my friends are rich but neither are intelligent. ✘
Either of the students is allowed to speak. ✔ Either of the students are allowed to speak. ✘
Neither of them wants to go to the party. ✔ Neither of them want to go to the party. ✘
Either of the cars is available for rent. ✔ Either of the cars are available for rent. ✘
Neither of the houses is for sale. ✔ Neither of the houses are for sale. ✘
Either option is acceptable. ✔ Either option are acceptable. ✘
Neither answer is correct. ✔ Neither answer are correct. ✘
Either choice is fine with me. ✔ Either choice are fine with me. ✘
Neither plan is working. ✔ Neither plan are working. ✘

Rule 11

यदि कोई भी दो Clauses Relative Pronoun (who, whose, whom, which, that) से जुड़े हुए हैं, तो वहाँ use होने वाली दूसरी verb antecedent (पूर्वपद) के अनुसार होगी।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
I who teach students am in trouble. ✔ I who teach students are in trouble. ✘
My father who helps people is a kind-hearted person. ✔ My father who helps people are a kind-hearted person. ✘
The teacher who guides us is absent today. ✔ The teacher who guides us are absent today. ✘
She who loves books is my friend. ✔ She who loves books are my friend. ✘
We who believe in justice are united. ✔ We who believes in justice is united. ✘
The dog that barks a lot is friendly. ✔ The dog that barks a lot are friendly. ✘
He who fights for others is brave. ✔ He who fights for others are brave. ✘
The woman who cooks dinner is my mother. ✔ The woman who cooks dinner are my mother. ✘
The child that plays outside is happy. ✔ The child that plays outside are happy. ✘

Rule 12

यदि कोई भी दो Clauses किसी भी Relative Pronoun से जुड़े हुए हो तथा Relative Pronoun के बाद कोई भी noun या pronoun use हो रहा हो, तो वहाँ use होने वाली पहली verb हमेशा उसी noun या pronoun के अनुसार होगी।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
He whose parents are rich is my friend. ✔ He whose parents is rich is my friend. ✘
He is one of the people who want to meet me. ✔ He is one of the people who wants to meet me. ✘
She whose brothers are soldiers is brave. ✔ She whose brothers is soldiers is brave. ✘
He is one of the students who work hard. ✔ He is one of the students who works hard. ✘
The teacher whose classes are popular is on leave. ✔ The teacher whose classes is popular is on leave. ✘
He is the only one of the players who wants to stay. ✔ He is the only one of the players who want to stay. ✘
The man whose children are successful is happy. ✔ The man whose children is successful is happy. ✘
The woman whose sons are doctors is proud. ✔ The woman whose sons is doctors is proud. ✘

Rule 13

यदि कोई भी दो Clauses किसी भी Relative Pronoun से जुड़े हुए हो तथा Relative Pronoun के तुरंत बाद verb use करनी हो, तो वह antecedent के अनुसार होगी।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
I who am your friend want to help him. ✔ I who is your friend want to help him. ✘
We who are your neighbors want to visit. ✔ We who is your neighbors want to visit. ✘
He who is my teacher has taught me well. ✔ He who am my teacher has taught me well. ✘
She who is the manager runs the company. ✔ She who are the manager runs the company. ✘
The students who are responsible will stay behind. ✔ The students who is responsible will stay behind. ✘
The child who is lost needs help. ✔ The child who are lost needs help. ✘
The people who are waiting need information. ✔ The people who is waiting need information. ✘

Rule 14

कुछ Nouns हमेशा Plural form में होती है तथा इनके साथ हमेशा Plural verb use की जाती है।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
My arrears have not been paid yet. ✔ My arrears has not been paid yet. ✘
The contents are interesting. ✔ The contents is interesting. ✘
His belongings were stolen. ✔ His belongings was stolen. ✘
All the surroundings are beautiful. ✔ All the surroundings is beautiful. ✘
The wages are low in this company. ✔ The wages is low in this company. ✘
Her earnings are sufficient. ✔ Her earnings is sufficient. ✘
The archives contain valuable documents. ✔ The archives contains valuable documents. ✘

Rule 15

कुछ nouns हमेशा singular uncountable noun के रूप में use होते हैं उनके साथ use होने वाली verb हमेशा singular होती है।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
The furniture has been moved. ✔ The furniture have been moved. ✘
The scenery is breathtaking. ✔ The scenery are breathtaking. ✘
The machinery is outdated. ✔ The machinery are outdated. ✘
His advice is helpful. ✔ His advice are helpful. ✘
The equipment is new. ✔ The equipment are new. ✘
The information was accurate. ✔ The information were accurate. ✘

Rule 16

यदि कोई भी subject preposition से बनाया जाता है उस sentence में use होने वाली verb हमेशा preposition के पहले use हुए भाग के according होगी।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
One of the students is going on a historical tour. ✔ One of the students are going on a historical tour. ✘
He with his friends is going to watch a movie. ✔ He with his friends are going to watch a movie. ✘
One of the books is missing. ✔ One of the books are missing. ✘
The boy with the girls was playing. ✔ The boy with the girls were playing. ✘
The teacher along with the students is coming. ✔ The teacher along with the students are coming. ✘

Rule 17

कुछ Collective nouns singular तथा Plural दोनों forms में एक जैसे ही use किये जाते हैं।

Correct Sentence Incorrect Sentence
The parliament has voted to pass the bill. ✔ The parliament have voted to pass the bill. ✘
The jury is deciding the case. ✔ The jury are deciding the case. ✘
The team has won the match. ✔ The team have won the match. ✘
The committee is in session. ✔ The committee are in session. ✘
The government has announced a new policy. ✔ The government have announced a new policy. ✘
